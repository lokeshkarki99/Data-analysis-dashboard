# Chocolate sales Data-Analysis using MS excel
## Objective: 
 This project focuses on analyzing chocolate sales data to help a business understand its performance, identify growth opportunities, and make data-driven decisions.
 Dataset 
<a href="https://github.com/lokeshkarki99/Data-analysis-dashboard/blob/main/Chocolate%20Sales.xlsx">Dataset</a>
## Business KPIs Solved
•	Total Sales Revenue – Overall business performance at a glance
•	Total Quantity Sold – Demand analysis for chocolate products
•	Top-Selling Products – Identifying best-performing chocolates
•	Region-wise Sales Performance – Market contribution by location
•	Top Customers – Customer contribution to revenue

Dashboard   <a href="https://github.com/lokeshkarki99/Data-analysis-dashboard/blob/main/Screenshot%20chocolate%20sales.png">Dashboard<a/>
## Tools & Techniques Used
•	Microsoft Excel
•	Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts
•	Slicers for interactive filtering
•	Data cleaning and formatting for accuracy
 ## Insights Generated
•	Identified high-revenue chocolate categories
•	Highlighted top regions driving maximum sales
•	Detected seasonal sales patterns
•	Helped prioritize key customers for business focus
